About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Cybersecurity at Korea University, affiliated with the Cryptographic Algorithm Lab. My research interests lie in side-channel analysis and post-quantum cryptography, with a focus on understanding and improving the practical security of cryptographic implementations against real-world leakage and emerging quantum-era threats.
